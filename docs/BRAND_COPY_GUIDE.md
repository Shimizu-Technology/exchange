# ExChange Brand Copy Guide (Approved Direction)

Last updated: 2026-03-06

## Core tone
- Playful, bold, and empowering
- Money-forward and cathartic (not cruel)
- Guam-local, conversational, easy to read

## Primary tagline
- **He bought it. You profit.**

## Auntie Daena-approved tagline pool
- He bought it. You profit.
- Breakup? Make it bank-up.
- From ex to extra cash.
- Moving on, making money.
- Eff that. Buy it.
- Your ex's trash, your treasure.

## Rotation set (live UI)
- Uses the full Auntie Daena-approved tagline pool above.

## Usage
- Use one primary tagline in key hero areas
- Rotate alternates in supporting surfaces (home side copy, social cards, empty states)
- Keep CTAs short and active

## CTA voice examples
- Cash out your ex
- Post your listing
- Tell the story
- Find your next steal

## Copy guardrails
- Keep it witty, not mean
- Avoid language that encourages harassment or revenge actions
- Keep ownership and safety language clear in legal/policy pages
